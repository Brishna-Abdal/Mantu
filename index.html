Mantu Introduction

Mantu is a traditional Afghan dumpling dish, often served with a flavorful yogurt and tomato sauce. It is a beloved dish, enjoyed during family gatherings and special occasions. The delicate dumplings, filled with spiced meat, symbolize the rich culinary heritage of Afghanistan.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 package wonton wrappers
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Step 1: Mix ground meat, onion, salt, and pepper.
  2. Step 2: Place a small spoonful of meat mixture in the center of each wonton wrapper.
  3. Step 3: Fold and seal the edges.
  4. Step 4: Steam for about 30 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Prepare sauce with yogurt, garlic, and tomato paste.
  6. Step 6: Serve mantu with sauce and garnish with cilantro.
